Our first picture together

Sophie and Alex met at Northwestern University in 2010. We aren't exactly sure how or when we were introduced to each other, but our best guess is that Andrew Brugman played a role in it. The real magic started during the summer of 2012 when we bothed worked at the Northwestern Office of Undergraduate Admission. We would give information sessions together, get lunch together, hang out after work. Then on August 10, 2012 we figured out that we both kinda liked each other.

August 10, 2012
